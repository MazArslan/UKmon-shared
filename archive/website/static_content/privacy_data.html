Privacy and Data Protection Policy

This page explains how we handle any personal and sensitive data that we collect.

Camera ID, name and geographic location of each camera.

We collect the precise geographic location of each camera, as well as its Camera ID and name for the following purposes:

  • For Scientific data analysis such as statistical reporting, orbit and trajectory calculations of meteors detected by the camera the results of which can be seen on the UK Meteor Network Archive website.
  • To include in the downloadable data made available from the website as part of our commitment to an open data policy.
  • To exchange data with other researchers and networks typically for the purpose of fireball analysis and strewn field identification.

    • This information will be retained indefinitely even if you leave the network.

    Name and email address.

    We collect your name and email address for the purpose of communication with you in relation to:

  • Operation of the network.
  • Notification of fireballs and potential falls.
  • Technical issues with your equipment or with the network.
  • Other purposes related to meteor observation and monitoring.

    • We will not use this information for any other purpose without your express consent and this information is not included in any downloadable data set or exchanged with any other network.
    If you leave the network we will remove your personal details from our systems.
