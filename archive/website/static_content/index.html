Welcome to the UKMON data archive.

Please use the menus above to browse or search the archive for data of interest.

The REPORTS menu gives you access to annual and shower reports, as well as trajectory and orbit reports for all matched events. You can also click on the year in the table below to go straight to an annual report for that year, or on the number in the Matches column to see details of events that we matched on more than one camera .

The DOWNLOAD menu includes data on both detections and matches. The data are in UFO compatible CSV format.

The SEARCH menu allows you to search for an event by date, and then download the results in various formats.

The data here are released under the CC BY 4.0 license, so if you are using the data whether for scientific or other purposes, your must reference this web site and UKMON in your work.
