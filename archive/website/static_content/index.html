Welcome to the UKMON data archive.

Please use the menus above to browse or search the archive for data of interest.

The REPORTS menu gives you access to annual and shower reports, as well as trajectory and orbit reports for all matched events. You can also click on the year in the table below to go straight to an annual report for that year.

The DOWNLOAD menu includes data on both detections and matches. The data are in UFO compatible CSV format.

The SEARCH menu provides the ability to search by date. From the search results page you can download all the data we have on each event including in formats other than UFO compatible CSV.

All the data here is freely available to download. However if you use data in any research or publication, you must attribute it to the UK Meteor Network.
