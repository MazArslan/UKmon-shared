Main Activity  Oct 14–31
Maximum        Oct 21-23
Peak ZHR       20-25
Radiant        06:20 +15.5°
Velocity       67km/sec 
Parent Body    1P/Halley

The Orionids are the second shower to originate from Comet Halley. They are fast meteors, 
generally best seen after midnight due to the location of the radiant which is 
closer to Gemini than Orion and doesn't rise till around 9pm local time. Orionids 
often have persistent trails.
